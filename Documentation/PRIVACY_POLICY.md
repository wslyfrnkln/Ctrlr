# Privacy Policy for Ctrlr

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## Overview

Ctrlr ("the App") is a MIDI controller application developed by SinAudio ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Ctrlr is designed to function entirely on your device without requiring:
- User accounts or registration
- Personal information
- Usage analytics or tracking
- Internet connectivity for core functionality

## How the App Works

Ctrlr communicates with Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) software via MIDI over Bluetooth. This communication:
- Occurs locally between your iPhone and your computer/MIDI device
- Does not pass through any external servers
- Does not involve data collection of any kind

## Permissions We Request

The App requests the following device permissions solely for MIDI functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Bluetooth | Required to connect to MIDI devices and DAW software |
| Microphone | Required by iOS for certain Bluetooth MIDI configurations |

These permissions are used exclusively for local MIDI communication and do not involve recording, storage, or transmission of audio or personal data.

## Data Storage

- All App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud storage or synchronization is used

## Third-Party Services

Ctrlr does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

Ctrlr does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
